ANG KAEW

RESERVOIR

"Ang Kaew Reservoir" located in front of Chiang Mai University. Constructed to supply water for the university, it is also a recreational area for Chiang Mai University students , campus residents and the local community.

CHIANG MAI UNIVERSITY

MAIN STADIUM

"Chiang Mai University Main Stadium" are located in the center of the university.It is a place for a student to do activity, e.g., outdoor sports, concerts, or other events and consists of a field or stage either partly or completely surrounded by a tiered structure designed to allow spectators to stand or sit and view the event.

THE CMU ART

MUSEUM

This museum is the main regional art museum in the north of Thailand. In addition to providing a venue for rotating contemporary art exhibitions by CMU art students and local, national and international artists, it is an education centre, and a centre for the preservation of art and culture of Chiang Mai and Northern Thailand, formerly named the Kingdom of Lanna. The Museum also organizes an annual international festival and publishes a two-monthly art magazine.
